The idea

A private curator for the classical canon.

Photograph what's in front of you — a window, a street, a cup of coffee. ClassicalLens reads the mood of the moment on your iPhone and answers with a single, considered classical piece, then opens it in Apple Music or Spotify. No playlists, no clutter — one confident match, with another reading a tap away.
